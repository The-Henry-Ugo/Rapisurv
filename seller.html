Seller Rapisurv Solutions

Selling Rapisurv Solutions

As a Rapisurv sales partner, you can manage the customer lifecycle, including sales, implementation consulting, and renewals. A reseller will manage a customer for sales and renewals while a value-added reseller (VAR) will manage sales, set-up consulting and renewals A partnership with us is an open door to new opportunities and markets. We rely on companies like yours to provide the coverage, local knowledge, and industry expertise. You will benefit from commissions for subscriptions or reselling rapisurv software.

Program Benefits

Isekit provides recurring benefits for sales partners of Rapisurv. standardized fees, discounts, and simplified contracts reduce administration and ensure the program grows along with your business, this is an engagement model that has deepening benefits, a points system that rewards you for business performance. To become and remain authorized to sell, you must pay annual program fees and meet minimum entry and ongoing requirements. 

Program Fees: $1000 per annum (Fee is currently waived, you can join for free) If your application is successful, we will guide you through each step of the onboarding process, during which time you’ll have to meet the following minimum entry requirements:

  • Pass due diligence check

  • Sign contract agreement

  • Submit business profile

  • Submit proof of industry network

  • Submit sales strategy document for Rapisurv

  • Some sales and solution trained employees (may be trained on solution after onboarding)
