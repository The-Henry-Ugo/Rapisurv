End-to-end solution that simplifies and streamlines the construction cost & contract management process.

Gain access to takeoff digitally without eliminating the use of standard methods of measurement (SMM).

Rapisurv works for both client side and contractor side of quantity surveying and cost engineering practices, it handles takeoff, billing, estimating, tender documentation, bidding, contract, variations management and valuations.

Rapisurv enables construction
management teams to do their jobs faster.

  • Takeoff with standard method of measurement

  • Create bill of quatities directly from takeoff

  • Estimate cost of construction

  • Progress valuation and certification

  • Variations management for scope changes
