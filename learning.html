Learn to use Rapisurv

Rapisurv have many integrated applications for various construction industry functions you may need to perform as a quantity surveyor, estimator, cost engineer, contract administrator, manager or as an owner of a construction business.

To help customers get the best out of Rapisurv, we have setup a special training program to give you comprehensive understand of rapisurv with options for remote and in-person classes


Options

In-person at office location - In-person training is available at our Lagos office. Classes are scheduled weekly, and slots assigned on first come first served basis. COVID-19 protocols are applied as dictated by local authorities.

In-person remotely - In-person training is available remotely via video conferencing. Classes are scheduled weekly, and slots assigned on first come first served basis.

How does it work?

  • Step 1: Sign up - Register for a class, choose your preferred start date and pay for your course, your place in the line will be reserved, our admin will work to get you into your preferred class if there are available slots, else you will be scheduled and asked to confirm.


  • Step 2: Class schedule - You will be notified of your class schedule, if the scheduled start date is same as your requested start date, you will receive a confirmation email. If not, you will be asked to confirm availability. You will get 30 days access to the learning system from your start date.


  • Step 3: Attend class - Attend your instructor led class to become an expert user of rapisurv. Coursework is 6 hours of instruction, 8 hours of self-paced practice.


  • Step 4: Practice -  You will receive access credentials to rapisurv practice application.


  • Step 5: Test -  We will test your knowledge after completion of the class and required minimum practice period.
